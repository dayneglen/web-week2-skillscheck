The More You Know

This my friends, is a pineapple. The more that you know about pineapples, the happier you will be. For example, did you know that pineapples don't ripen significantly after harvest? Amazing! Or that pineapples are actually a result of 100 to 200 individual flowers fusing together. Spectacular!! Just make sure you know: One pineapple plant produces only one pineapple per season. So respect your pinapples! It's the only one that plant had!
